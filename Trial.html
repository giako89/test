Need Help?

We want to get to know you better and we hope that you want to learn more about Elmira College. By completing the information request form we will add your name to our mailing list. Please include your name, address, phone number, secondary school, and your academic and extracurricular interests. You should begin to receive mail from us within the next two to four weeks. If our purple and gold materials do not begin to appear in your mailbox, please let us know so that we can correct the problem. We look forward to getting to know you better.
